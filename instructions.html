How do I order?

Sizing tutorial

To make sure your press-on nails fit perfectly, we’ll need a few reference photos of your natural nails.

Just like the example below, take three photos for each hand: one of the thumb, one of the index and middle fingers together, and one of the ring and pinky together.

Please include a coin next to your nails in each photo for scale.

Accepted coins: 50 bani, 1 euro, 2 euro, 50 eurocent, U.S. nickel, or U.S. quarter.

Photo guidelines:

  • Take your photos in good lighting (natural light is best).
  • Make sure they are clear, sharp, and unfiltered.
  • Avoid harsh shadows or reflections.
  • The coin should appear as a perfect circle, not oval (to ensure the right angle).
  • Keep the coin close to your nails in each shot.

Keep in mind that measurements may slightly differ in real life! Therefore, I reccomend also sending measurements made with the tape method.

  1. Cut a small piece of scotch tape and place it across the base of your nail, covering the full width.
  2. Use a pen or fine pencil to mark the edges of your nail on the tape (from one side to the other).
  3. Peel the tape off carefully and place it on a ruler, measuring the distance between the two marks in millimeters (mm).

Once you have your measurements, send me a DM with your nail sizes (or your reference photos), the length, shape, and design you’d like!
From there, we’ll chat and create your dream set together!
